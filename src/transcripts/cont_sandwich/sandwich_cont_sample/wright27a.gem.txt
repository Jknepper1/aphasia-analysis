you want me to tell you how to make a peanut butter and jelly sandwich ?
so you hafta get two pieces of bread peanut butter and jelly and a knife .
you hafta put the two pieces of bread down on the counter or plate or something or other .
open the jar of peanut butter .
take the peanut butter out .
spread it out on the piece of bread .
do the same thing for the jelly .
open the jar of jelly .
use the knife to take the jelly out .
spread it across the other piece of bread .
and put the two pieces of bread together with the jelly and peanut butter facing each other .
put the lids back on the jars and knife in the sink and you're done .
